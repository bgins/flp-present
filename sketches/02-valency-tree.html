§ 2::admissibility

A run is admissible provided that at most one process is faulty and that all messages sent to nonfaulty processes are eventually received.

§ 3::valency

For a configuration C, let V be the set of decision values of configurations reachable from C. C is bivalent if |V| = 2.

[ paper §3 ]
▸ the tree on the right is V made visible — the leaves are the decisions every continuation eventually reaches. Both 0 and 1 appear: |V| = 2.
